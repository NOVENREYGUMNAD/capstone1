Jose Mari Chan

Filipino Singer-songwriter

Jose Mari Chan is a renowned Filipino singer, songwriter, and businessman. He is often referred to as the "Father of Philippine Christmas Music" due to his popular Christmas songs that have become classics in the Philippines. Chan's career spans several decades, and his soothing voice and timeless hits have made him a beloved figure in Filipino music. Apart from his musical success, he is also known for his successful ventures in the business world.
